Frequently Asked Questions

What is MySeat?

MySeat is your all-in-one hub for discovering restaurants, placing orders, and booking catering services with ease.

How do I place an order?

Simply visit our Order page, browse the featured restaurants, and click on your preferred place to view their menu and place your order.

Can I book catering for events?

Yes! Head to our Caterers page to explore available services, view details, and contact your preferred provider directly.

Is there a delivery charge?

Delivery fees vary by restaurant and will be displayed during checkout before confirming your order.
